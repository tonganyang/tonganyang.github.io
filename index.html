Biography

I am a M.E student in Computer Vision Group (CVG), under the supervision of A.Prof. Chao Tang (唐超) . I received the B.E. degree from Nanyang Institute of Technology ( NYIST ) in 2020. My research interests include deep learning and computer vision. Specifically, I focus on Semi-supervised Learning (SSL) and Human Action Recognition (HAR).
